Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a famous North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried, fluffy bread called bhature. This dish is usually enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner and is a favorite in many households and restaurants. It’s a delightful combination of flavors and textures, with the rich and spicy chole paired with crispy bhature for an irresistible meal.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Indian vegetarian main course chickpeas

instructions

step 1

Soak the chickpeas overnight or for at least 6 hours. Drain and rinse the chickpeas before cooking.

step 2

In a pressure cooker, add the soaked chickpeas, water, salt, and a tea bag (optional) to give the chole a dark color. Cook for 4-5 whistles and set aside.

step 3

In a pan, heat some oil and sauté onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Once the onions become translucent, add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and red chili powder. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.

step 4

Add the cooked chickpeas to the pan and mix well. Let it cook for 15-20 minutes. Adjust salt and add water as needed to achieve your desired consistency.

step 5

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve hot with bhature.

step 6

For the bhature: Combine flour, yeast, sugar, salt, and baking soda. Add warm water to form a dough and let it rise for 2 hours. Once the dough is ready, roll out the dough into round discs and deep-fry them until golden and puffed.

ingredients

2 cups chickpeas (soaked)

1 onion, chopped

2 tomatoes, chopped

1-inch ginger, grated

2-3 garlic cloves, minced

1-2 green chilies, chopped

1 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp cumin powder

1 tsp coriander powder

1 tsp garam masala

1 tsp red chili powder

2 tbsp oil

Salt to taste

Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

1 lemon for garnish

For Bhature:

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp yeast

1 tsp sugar

1/2 tsp baking soda

Warm water to knead

Oil for deep frying

tools

Pressure Cooker

Frying Pan

Rolling Pin

Deep Fryer or Pan for Frying

Spatula
