Vegetable Soup

Vegetable Soup is a healthy and comforting dish made with a variety of fresh vegetables and aromatic herbs. This light, nutritious soup is perfect for a warm and satisfying meal. Whether you are looking to detox, stay healthy, or enjoy a cozy bowl on a chilly evening, this vegetable soup is an ideal choice. Packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals, it's a wholesome meal that everyone can enjoy.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : vegetarian healthy soup low-calorie

instructions

step 1

Wash and chop the vegetables (carrot, celery, potatoes, onions, tomatoes, and spinach) into small pieces.

step 2

In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger, and saut√© until fragrant.

step 3

Add chopped vegetables (carrot, celery, potato, and tomatoes) to the pot. Stir well and cook for 5 minutes.

step 4

Pour in vegetable broth or water, add salt, pepper, and any herbs like thyme or rosemary. Bring to a boil.

step 5

Once the soup boils, reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

step 6

Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. You can also add a splash of lemon juice or vinegar for extra freshness.

ingredients

1 carrot, chopped

2 celery stalks, chopped

2 medium potatoes, chopped

1 onion, chopped

2 tomatoes, chopped

1 cup spinach leaves

2 cloves garlic, minced

1-inch piece of ginger, grated

5 cups vegetable broth (or water)

1 tbsp olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary (optional)

Lemon juice (optional)

tools

Large Soup Pot

Chopping Knife

Cutting Board

Wooden Spoon

Ladle
