Poha

Poha is a popular Indian breakfast made with flattened rice, peanuts, spices, and vegetables. It’s a quick, healthy, and flavorful dish that can be served with a sprinkle of fresh coriander leaves. The soft texture of the poha combined with the crunch of peanuts and the aromatic spices makes it a wholesome start to your day.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : breakfast vegetarian healthy Indian

instructions

step 1

Rinse the poha (flattened rice) in water and drain it completely. Set it aside for a few minutes.

step 2

Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.

step 3

Add finely chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onions become translucent.

step 4

Add the chopped potatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the potatoes are soft.

step 5

Add the rinsed poha to the pan and mix gently. Let it cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

step 6

Garnish with chopped coriander leaves, peanuts, and a squeeze of lemon juice.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups poha (flattened rice)

1 small onion, finely chopped

1 potato, chopped

1/4 cup peanuts

1 green chili, chopped

1 tsp mustard seeds

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

1 tbsp oil

Salt to taste

Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Lemon juice to taste

tools

Frying Pan

Spatula

Chopping Board

Knife
